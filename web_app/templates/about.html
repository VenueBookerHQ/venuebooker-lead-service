About Us

Venuebooker operates from its head office in Belfast, Northern Ireland. Venuebooker is a bespoke venue booking web portal which provides consumers with a simple, fast and reliable way to find, compare and book the perfect venue for any occasion, without the traditional hassle of multiple time- consuming phone calls followed by the review of an array of different venues. Venuebooker has everything you needed to complete all their events and function requirements online or on their smartphone, whilst venue managers in restaurants, hotels and event spaces now have a platform to showcase and sell all their event space and related proudest and services.
